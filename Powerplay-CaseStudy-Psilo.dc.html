Psilo

Our pro-bono passion project — a lifestyle brand we built from a single logo mark into an ever-growing run of seasonal packaging.

CLIENT
Psilo Lifestyle Products
SCOPE
Logo · Packaging · Illustration
YEAR
Ongoing
ENGAGEMENT
Pro bono
A PRO-BONO
PASSION PROJECT

Psilo is the fun one — a lifestyle brand we took on pro bono, and the project where we get to be a little louder, weirder, and more hands-on than the day job usually allows.

It started with one ask — a logo mark — and never really stopped. Today we design seasonal packaging across a growing family of products, treating every drop as its own small world to illustrate.

01
Situation

A playful brand with no face.

Psilo arrived with personality to spare and nothing to wear it on. They needed a mark that could read premium on a foil-stamped chocolate bar and still feel playful on a bag of gummies — without fracturing into two different brands.

The answer was a confident, rounded wordmark: geometric enough to anchor a whole system, friendly enough to never take itself too seriously.

02
Action

One mark, many moods.

With the logo set, we treated packaging as a flexible canvas rather than a fixed template. The mark stays constant; the world around it shifts with every release.

Monochrome, silver-foil nature illustrations give the chocolate line a transcendent, collectible feel. The gummies run the other direction — saturated, psychedelic, and loud. Same brand, completely different mood.

03
Output & Results

A shelf that keeps growing.

What began as one logo is now a full seasonal catalog — Psychedelic Chocolate, Psychedelic Gummies, Lovecakes, and one-off drops like Lemonheadz — each carrying its own illustrated identity while staying unmistakably Psilo.

Because it's pro bono and personal, Psilo stays a playground — a place to push typography, illustration, and packaging ideas that keep the rest of our work sharp.
